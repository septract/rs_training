The department of Electronics will hold a poster competition on 8th May 2013, to which 2nd year CS students are invited to contribute.

To print your poster, please send it to Owen Francis (owen.francis@york.ac.uk) for printing. The deadline to send your posters to Owen for printing is Monday 22 April 2013.

There will be cash prizes available for the best posters in the following categories - 1st Prize £150, 2nd Prize £100, 3rd Prize £75, 4th Prize £50, 5th Prize £25.

Computer Science posters will judged by judges from Computer Science on the following assessment criteria:
  1. Clarity (total of 10 marks)
  2. Communication of key aspects (total of 10 marks)
  3. Overall design (total of 10 marks)

The posters will be judged on Wednesday 8 May 2013 followed by a drinks reception in room PT005A at 5.15pm during which the prizes will be awarded.
